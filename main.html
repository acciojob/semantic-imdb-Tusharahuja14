Inception

Actors Quotes

Inception is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie explores the concept of dream manipulation, where skilled individuals infiltrate others' dreams to extract or plant information.

Main Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state.

Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Cobb's trusted associate and expert in dream architecture.

Elliot Page as Ariadne, a talented architecture student who helps Cobb navigate the dream world.

Favorite Quote

"You mustn’t be afraid to dream a little bigger, darling." – Eames

This quote embodies the movie's imaginative approach to exploring the limits of human dreams and creativity.
